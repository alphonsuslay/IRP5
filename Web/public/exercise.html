Exercise

Cardio

Warm-Up : 5-10mins

• Jumping Jacks: 1 minute

• High knees: 1 minute

• Butt kicks: 1 minute

• Jump rope (or imaginary jump rope): 3 minutes

• Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists): 5 minutes

Exercises : Pick 3-4, each 20-30 secs, rest 10-15 seconds between each

• Running in Place

• Jumping Lunges

• High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) Sprints

Sprint at maximum effort for 20-30 seconds.
Walk or jog in place for 10-15 seconds as active recovery.
Repeat the cycle for 5-10 rounds.

• Burpees

• Mountain Climbers

Cool Down : 5-10 minutes

• Brisk walking or slow jogging: 3-5 minutes

• Static stretches (hamstring stretch, calf stretch, quad stretch, hip flexor stretch): 5 minutes

• Deep diaphragmatic breathing and relaxation exercises.

Strength Training

Warm-Up : 5-10mins

• Light cardiovascular exercise (brisk walking, jogging, or jump rope): 5 minutes

• Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, hip circles): 5 minutes

Exercises : 3 sets of 10-12 reps per exercise

• Squats

• Push-ups

• Dumbbell Lunges

Hold a dumbbell in each hand.
Step forward with one foot and lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.
Push back to the starting position and repeat on the other leg.

• Dumbbell Rows

Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing your body.
Bend at the hips, keeping your back straight.
Pull the dumbbells to your sides, squeezing your shoulder blades.
Lower the weights back to the starting position.

• Bench Press

• Plank : Hold for 30-60 seconds

Cool Down : 5-10 minutes

• Static stretches for major muscle groups (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, back).

• Deep diaphragmatic breathing and relaxation exercises.
